About Us

Get to know the story, values, and strengths of ASBCS

Company Snapshot

Abergas Sajise Bookkeeping & Consultancy Services (ASBCS) is a trusted provider of bookkeeping, tax compliance, and financial consultancy solutions. Since 2015, ASBCS has empowered MSMEs, cooperatives, and startups across the Philippines with reliable financial systems and advisory services.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower businesses through accurate bookkeeping, compliant tax practices, and strategic financial consulting — enabling them to focus on growth and sustainability.

Our Vision

To be the premier partner for MSMEs and organizations in the Philippines seeking financial stability, compliance, and long-term growth.

Governance & Personnel

ASBCS is managed by a team of dedicated professionals with extensive experience in bookkeeping, accounting systems, taxation, and financial consultancy. We prioritize integrity, transparency, and compliance with Philippine accounting and tax standards.

Competitive Strengths

  • ✔ Strong expertise in bookkeeping and financial reporting compliant with IFRS/PAS.
  • ✔ Comprehensive tax compliance and advisory services.
  • ✔ Proven track record in supporting MSMEs, startups, and cooperatives.
  • ✔ Skilled in computerized accounting system setup and data migration.
  • ✔ Personalized consulting and training programs tailored to client needs.
