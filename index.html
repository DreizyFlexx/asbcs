Who We Are

Abergas Sajise Bookkeeping & Consultancy Services (ASBCS) is dedicated to helping businesses thrive through accurate bookkeeping, tax compliance, and financial consultancy. Since 2015, we have been empowering MSMEs, cooperatives, and startups in Manila and beyond with reliable and professional support.

Our Core Services

Comprehensive solutions to support your business growth and compliance

Bookkeeping & Record-Keeping

From manual to computerized transitions, journals, ledgers, and trial balances — ensuring accurate and organized financial data.

Accounting System Setup

Chart of accounts design and software implementation to modernize and streamline your accounting processes.

Financial Statement Preparation

Preparation of compliant financial reports aligned with IFRS and PAS, tailored for better decision-making.

Tax Compliance & Advisory

Timely filing, tax planning, and advisory services to help you comply with Philippine laws and avoid penalties.

Payroll & Statutory Remittances

Accurate payroll processing and remittances for SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG, and BIR obligations.

Consulting, Training & Special Projects

Staff training, record clean-up, data migration, and audit support designed to strengthen your financial system.
